Churches Together Winchester

A focus for Christians in Winchester.

Church Together Winchester provides a focus for Christians in Winchester to work together to enrich their faith and to bear witness by various initiatives of social outreach.

By better understanding of different Christian traditions, member churches hope to move ever closer to visible unity.
